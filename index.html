What does open carry mean?

Texans can carry their guns in plain view in a shoulder or belt holster anywhere that is not expressly prohibited by law.

Governor Greg Abbott signed House Bill 910 on January 1, 2016 which allowed open carry in Texas.

Can private properties prohibit open carry?

The owner can post a PC 30.07 sign that bans open carry.

A PC 30.07 sign must include the following language:

In English

Pursuant to Section 30.07, Penal Code (Trespass by license holder with an openly carried handgun), a person licensed under Subchapter H, Chapter 411, Government Code (handgun licensing law), may not enter this property with a handgun that is carried openly.
