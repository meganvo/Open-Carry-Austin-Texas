Megan Vo, Product Designer

Currently

I lead design at RigUp where we're designing a platform to foster a safer, efficient, and transparent oil industry.


Outside of RigUp, I enjoy learning about front-end web development through (too personal) side projects, exploring web typography, listening to music that makes me cry, reading graphic novels, and eating a dumb amount of hot dogs.
