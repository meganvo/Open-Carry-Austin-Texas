About Me

I'm a product designer living and working in Austin, Texas. I've had previous experience in advertising agencies, startups, and freelancing. I went to school for advertising but realized I was passionate about crafting intuitive user experiences rather than a snappy tagline. I think a lot about diversity within the tech industry (especially how it intersects with the oil industry) & ways to improve this in my environment.

Lately, I've been dividing my time between learning about front-end web development through (too personal) side projects, exploring web typography, learning how to play the drums without disturbing my roommates, and eating a dumb amount of hot dogs.
