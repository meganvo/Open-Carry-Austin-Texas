Guiding principles

Go wide, go deep

Problems are messy. Ask a lot of questions to understand root issues before jumping to solutions.

Data-informed, not data-driven

Data can be really powerful in guiding decisions and bringing context to problems. However, the data that we choose and how it's measured is systemically biased.

Be vulnerable, stay humble
Reflective

When you’re done you think of what you missed of what could be better and you ask questions again
