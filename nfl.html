The NFL wanted a way to engage their fans during the draft season - when activity levels decline. I designed an experience where fans could pit players head to head and compare their social media stats.

Adding Players

Mobile-first...not just a buzzword

My initial iterations split up the actions of adding topics and viewing the p. However, I quickly learned of mobile wires, I realized a better use of space would be to combine the actions into one view.

It wasn't a time-efficient user experience to input a topic and have to scroll all the way down to view stats and then scroll up to view the graph.

Onboarding

Top of mind was prioritizing that this was a unique experience to NFL.com visitors. They needed to quickly understand from the get-go what this was and how to use it.

The player leaderboard provided users ideas to quickly get started.
