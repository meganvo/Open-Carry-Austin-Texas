NFL wanted a way to engage their fans during the draft season when scheduled games weren't in effect. I designed an experience where fans could pit players head to head and compare their social media stats.

Onboarding

This was a unique experience for users visiting the experience. They needed to quickly understand from the beginning what this was and how to use it.

Mobile first design matters

My initial iterations split up the actions of adding topics and viewing the statistics. However, I quickly learned after my initial mobile wires iterations that there was a lack of instant feedback validation once a user inputted a topic. Data would populate below the fold and it wasn't clear to the user that an action occured.

It wasn't an intutive or efficient UX to input a topic and have to scroll all the way down to view stats and then scroll up to view the graph.
